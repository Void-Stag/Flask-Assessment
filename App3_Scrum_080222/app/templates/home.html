Certified Scrum Master (CSM)

 

 
 

Introduction

The Scrum Master is an examplary leader, one who serves and leads the team. The certification process provides candidates with an understanding of Scrum values, which emphasize teamwork, accountability and iterative progress toward a well-defined goal. 

 
 
 
 

CSM Certifications Benefits

The Agile methodology is taking the world by storm, and the CSM credential can set you apart  from the crowd. These credentials show that you're a leader who can provide expertise far beyond what a conventional project manager could contribute.

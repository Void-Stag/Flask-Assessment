Different types of Development Cycles

Trainer: Jane Dane

The product development cycle is the process of taking a product from an idea through its market release and beyond. This cycle involves many departments in a company: product managers, developers, designers, QA testers, and others.
Development strategies fall under many names that include spiral, waterfall, agile, XP, rapid prototyping to name but a few. The common goal of all these strategies or software development plans is to develop a product. This course will teach you more about that.

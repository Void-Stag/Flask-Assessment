Introduction of scrum.

Scrum is a framework within which people can address complex adaptive problems, while productively and creatively delivering products of the highest possible value. The framework is designed to be lightweight and simple to understand, while still remaining robust enough to support complex product development in the face of rapidly changing business environments.
